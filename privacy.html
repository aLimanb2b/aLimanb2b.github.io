Privacy Policy

Last updated: [18/12/25]

This Privacy Policy describes how BOXTOBOX LTD ("Company", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and discloses personal data of users (each, a "User") of the Box to Box mobile application (the "App").

This Policy is issued in compliance with the relevant data protection laws and applies solely to the Box to Box App and services offered through it.

1. Personal Data We Collect

We collect limited personal data only, namely:

  • Name
  • Country
  • Email address

This information is collected when a user registers for an event through the App.

We do not collect:

  • phone numbers;
  • payment or financial information;
  • precise location data;
  • biometric data; or
  • sensitive personal data.

3. How We Collect Personal Data

Personal data is collected directly from users when they voluntarily submit their information through the App’s registration process.

The App does not permit anonymous event registration.

4. Lawful Basis for Processing

We process personal data in accordance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act, 2023 on the following lawful bases:

  • Performance of a contract – to enable event registration and participation.
  • Consent – where users voluntarily provide their name and email address.
  • Legitimate interests – limited to managing events and communicating essential event-related information, provided such interests do not override users’ rights.

5. Purposes of Processing

We use collected personal data strictly to:

  • register users for events;
  • communicate important event-related updates;
  • manage attendance and event administration; and
  • comply with legal or regulatory obligations.

We do not use personal data for targeted advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to administer the relevant event and meet legal or regulatory requirements.

After completion of the event, personal data may be deleted or anonymised unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal data.

Personal data may be shared only with:

  • authorised service providers assisting with event management; or
  • regulatory or law enforcement authorities where required by law.

All third parties, where engaged, are bound by appropriate data protection obligations.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to safeguard personal data against unauthorised access, loss, misuse, alteration, or disclosure.

Access to personal data is limited to authorised personnel strictly on a need-to-know basis.

9. Data Subject Rights

In accordance with the applicable laws users have the right to:

  • access their personal data;
  • request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data;
  • request deletion of personal data where lawful;
  • withdraw consent at any time;
  • object to certain processing activities; and
  • lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC).

Requests may be submitted via Boxtobox.me@gmail.com and will be addressed within the statutory timeframe.

10. Children’s Data

The Box-to-Box App is not intended for persons under the age of 18.

We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. Where processing of children’s data is required by law, verifiable parental or guardian consent shall be obtained in accordance with the applicable laws.

11. Cross-Border Data Transfers

Personal data is stored in Firebase and processed in Nigeria. Where cross-border transfers become necessary, such transfers shall be carried out in accordance with the safeguards required under the relevant laws.

12. Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that poses a risk to users’ rights and freedoms:

  • the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) will be notified within 72 hours; and
  • affected users will be informed without undue delay where required by law.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may modify this Privacy Policy at any time in the future. Where this happens, we will communicate the changes to you. Material changes will be communicated through the App or other appropriate means.

Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
